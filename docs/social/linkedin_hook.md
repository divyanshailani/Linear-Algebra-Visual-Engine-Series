## Short post draft

I built a master showcase for the first phase of my Simulation Architect path:
the **Linear Algebra Visual Engine Series**.

It is a 5-project series inspired by 3Blue1Brown's *Essence of Linear Algebra*,
where I try to prove hard linear algebra ideas by building them as real engines
in Python, NumPy, and Blender `bpy`.

The series covers:
- linear transformations
- 3D matrices
- change of basis
- Cramer's Rule
- eigenvectors

Each project follows the same rule:
learn the concept, prove it in code, verify it, then render it cinematically.

Master repo:
https://github.com/divyanshailani/Linear-Algebra-Visual-Engine-Series
